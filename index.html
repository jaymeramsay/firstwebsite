The Life and Times of Jayme.

This is me!

I was born in Atlanta, Georgia in 1982, but grew up in the suburbs. I'm a big sister. I was named after Jaime Summers, the Bionic Woman. I taught myself how to play the drums when I was eleven. I moved to Austin on New Year's 2005 where I started a career in theater. I was also a pre-K montessori school teacher for five years. I currently reside in Barton Hills. I have no pets. Oh! And I recently found my father :)

Music and theater are passions of mine. I'm a performance artist with copious amounts of experience in many forms: burlesque, mime, clown, mask, scripted, unscripted/improvised, and puppetry. I can't stand cilantro or licorice. I don't like the Beatles, but Huey Lewis and the News are pretty great. I suck at gardening and geography, but I'm really good at dancing and conversing. I enjoy gaming. I'm a leo. My house is Gryffindor. I'm an elf and a jedi. I'm more magic than melee. I love science!

I'm in a band!

We are a puppet-fronted emo band called Fragile Rock. I play drums! We've been together almost three years, and just cut our first full length album last month. We also debuted SXSW 2017 and appeared on NPR's Tiny Desk Concert.

Talk to me about Music! I absolutely love it.

Allow me to introduce Marc!

Marc is my heart. We're pretty gross together. This October will be our 5th anniversary. Together we've done many things: travel, perform on stage, direct improvised plays, host events, run and play games, protest, and a lot more. If it wasn't for him, I wouldn't be here at Galvanize pursuing a career in software engineering. He has given me enough encouragement and confidence to make such a big change in my life, and well, he kinda does this a lot for me. Thank you, Marc!

Looking forward to working with all of you and getting to know you better! Hello, World!
